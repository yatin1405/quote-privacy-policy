Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Quote App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Quote App does not require user registration and does not collect personal information during normal usage.

We may collect the following information only when you choose to contact us:

  • App Version
  • Android OS Version
  • Device Name / Model
  • Email Address (only if you send us an email via Contact Us)

2. How Information Is Collected

When you tap the Contact Us option, the app opens the Gmail application with the above device information automatically added to the email body. You manually type your message and send the email.

We do not automatically collect or transmit this information without your action.

3. Quotes and Remote Configuration

Quote App fetches quotes from a remote configuration service to display content inside the app. These quotes are static text and are not linked to any personal user data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your questions or feedback
  • To identify and fix app issues
  • To improve app stability and user experience

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Any information shared via email is used strictly for support and communication purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for core functionality:

  • Google Play Store – for app rating (Rate Us)
  • Android Sharing Intent – for sharing the app (Share App)
  • Gmail App – for sending feedback emails (Contact Us)

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Quote App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Users are advised to review this policy periodically.
